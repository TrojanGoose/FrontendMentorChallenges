Duapa

App is digital marketing platform and knowledge center for smallholder farmers and service providers, The App is the most secured digital platform, where farmers get to buy and sell their products to customers. The App has a secured payment platforms for farmers to buy products, sell products, make payments, receive payment, and get micro credits, production and research information. Farmers can now have access to advisory and extension services, access to crop and harvest insurance, Pets and disease control, climate information and customer help.

"Farming by all, for all"

BEIT FARMS is the home of smallholder farmers in Africa, we manage 60, 000 + smallholder farmers with 80% being women and youth. We have organized over 10 different training programs for our smallholder farmers in the areas of Digital empowerment, effective marketing skills, Climate change, e-commerce, financial management, improved technology, rural community development. We continue to use our collective innovation idea to create a sustainable digital world for smallholder farmers to increase production, make good incomes, improve their livelihoods and look after their families We are focus on Digitization of African agriculture to keep pace with globe food systems
